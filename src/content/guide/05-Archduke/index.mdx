# Archduke/Archduchess Rank Guide

Archduke and Archduchess rank represents the highest noble authority below royalty. You're among the most powerful beings in the realm.

## Areas Available

### Fiery Warpath
The treacherous Fiery Warpath offers:
- Fire-based mechanics
- War-themed challenges
- Fiery mouse types

### Living Garden
The mystical Living Garden provides:
- Life-based mechanics
- Growth and decay systems
- Living plant challenges

## Archducal Power

1. **Command armies** - Lead hunting expeditions
2. **Shape policy** - Influence hunting laws
3. **Protect the realm** - Defend against threats

---

*This guide covers both Archduke and Archduchess hunters - gameplay mechanics are identical regardless of gender.*
