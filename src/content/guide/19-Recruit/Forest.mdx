# Recruit Rank Guide

Welcome to Recruit rank! You've completed your basic training and are ready to explore the wider world of MouseHunt.

## Areas Available

### Meadow
The peaceful Meadow is your first hunting ground as a Recruit. Here you'll learn about:
- Basic mouse types
- Simple bait strategies
- Your first upgrades

### Harbour
The bustling Harbour introduces you to:
- Water-based hunting
- Different mouse behaviors
- Basic trap mechanics

## Tips for Recruit Hunters

1. **Focus on completing quests** - They provide essential upgrades
2. **Save your gold** - Don't spend on unnecessary items yet
3. **Join a team** - Early teamwork can accelerate your progress

---

*This guide covers both Recruit hunters regardless of gender - gameplay mechanics are identical for all players.*
