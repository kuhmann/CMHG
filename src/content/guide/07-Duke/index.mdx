# Duke/Duchess Rank Guide

Duke and Duchess rank represents the pinnacle of noble authority. You're among the most powerful hunters in the realm.

## Areas Available

### Bristle Woods
The mysterious Bristle Woods offer:
- Thorn-based mechanics
- Forest survival challenges
- Bristle mouse types

### Whisker Woods
The ancient Whisker Woods provide:
- Ancient forest secrets
- Whisker-based mechanics
- Historical challenges

## Ducal Authority

1. **Command respect** - Your word carries great weight
2. **Lead councils** - Help shape hunting policy
3. **Protect traditions** - Preserve the ancient hunting ways

---

*This guide covers both Duke and Duchess hunters - gameplay mechanics are identical regardless of gender.*
