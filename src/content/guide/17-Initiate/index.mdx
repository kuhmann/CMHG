# Initiate Rank Guide

Initiate rank marks the beginning of specialized hunting paths and more complex challenges.

## Areas Available

### Desert
The harsh Desert environment teaches:
- Resource management
- Heat-based mechanics
- Sand mouse behaviors

### Jungle
The mysterious Jungle introduces:
- Poison mechanics
- Hidden passages
- Jungle-specific mice

## Initiate Techniques

1. **Specialize your approach** - Focus on one area type at a time
2. **Build your collection** - Start gathering area-specific items
3. **Learn from failures** - Every failed hunt teaches you something

---

*This guide covers both Initiate hunters regardless of gender - gameplay mechanics are identical for all players.*
