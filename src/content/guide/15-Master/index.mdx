# Master Rank Guide

Master rank represents true expertise in hunting. You've proven your skills and are ready for the most challenging areas.

## Areas Available

### Catacombs
The ancient Catacombs challenge you with:
- Undead mouse types
- Dark environment mechanics
- Historical hunting lore

### Library
The vast Library offers:
- Knowledge-based hunting
- Rare manuscript mice
- Intellectual challenges

## Master Techniques

1. **Perfect your timing** - Master the art of precise hunting
2. **Optimize your loadout** - Every piece of equipment matters
3. **Lead others** - Share your knowledge with newer hunters

---

*This guide covers all Master hunters regardless of gender - gameplay mechanics are identical for all players.*
