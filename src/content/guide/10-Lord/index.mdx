# Lord/Lady Rank Guide

Lord and Lady rank represents nobility and leadership. You're a ruler of your own hunting domain.

## Areas Available

### Fungal Cavern
The mysterious Fungal Cavern offers:
- Spore-based mechanics
- Underground exploration
- Fungal mouse types

### Labyrinth
The complex Labyrinth provides:
- Maze navigation mechanics
- Hidden passage discovery
- Labyrinth-specific challenges

## Noble Responsibilities

1. **Rule wisely** - Make strategic decisions about your hunting path
2. **Lead by example** - Show others the way forward
3. **Build your legacy** - Create a lasting impact on the community

---

*This guide covers both Lord and Lady hunters - gameplay mechanics are identical regardless of gender.*
