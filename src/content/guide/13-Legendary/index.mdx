# Legendary Rank Guide

Legendary rank is the stuff of legends. You've achieved what few hunters ever accomplish.

## Areas Available

### Floating Islands
The mystical Floating Islands offer:
- Gravity-defying mechanics
- Cloud-based hunting
- Legendary mouse types

### Crystal Cave
The beautiful Crystal Cave provides:
- Crystal-based mechanics
- Light and shadow hunting
- Rare gem mice

## Legendary Achievements

1. **Complete all areas** - Prove your mastery everywhere
2. **Collect rare items** - Build the ultimate collection
3. **Write your own legend** - Create strategies others will follow

---

*This guide covers all Legendary hunters regardless of gender - gameplay mechanics are identical for all players.*
